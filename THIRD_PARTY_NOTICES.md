# Third-Party Notices

## Natural Resources Canada vehicle data

The offline vehicle catalogue is derived from the Government of Canada **Fuel consumption ratings** dataset, updated through model year 2026.

- Source: https://open.canada.ca/data/en/dataset/98f1a129-f628-4ce4-b24d-6f16bf24dd64
- Provider: Natural Resources Canada
- Licence: Open Government Licence - Canada

The source provides standardized fuel-consumption ratings. Lakshmi does not represent those ratings as guaranteed real-world performance. Observed figures are calculated separately from the user's own fill-up history.
